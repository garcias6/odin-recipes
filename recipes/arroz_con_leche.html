Arroz Con Leche

Description

Arroz Con Leche is a sweet dish, often called "Mexican Rice Pudding." It can be eaten as a dessert, or even as a breakfast if you like loads of sugar first thing in the morning.

For the most part, this recipe is highly customizable. You can make this as sweet (or not) as you want. The amoung of cinnamon can also be changed to your liking. Some people also enjoy adding extras to the dish such as nuts or raisins. Really, you can make this however you like.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • granulated sugar
  • evaporated milk
  • ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. cook white rice as normal with no seasoning
  2. once rice is fully cooked, add condensed milk to cover rice and continue to simmer
  3. add sugar to taste, stirring frequently
  4. add cinnamon to taste, stir
  5. remove from heat
