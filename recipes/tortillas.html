Tortillas

Description

Tortillas are a simple, yet versitile side dish for almost any meal. They can be eaten on their own with butter, they can be used in quesadillas, or they can be eaten with a meal. The sky's the limit!

They can be a bit complicated to get perfect, but if you keep practicing, you will soon be making them by the dozen!

Ingredients

  • white bread flour - 4 cups
  • baking powder - 1 tsp
  • table salt - 1 tsp
  • lard or vegetable shortning - .5 cup
  • water - 1 cup

Steps

  1. mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. mix in wet ingredients until dough is formed
  3. roll bits of dough into small balls
  4. flatten balls and roll into circles with rolling pin
  5. cook flattened tortillas on griddle on medium heat
  6. flip when bubbles appear, then remove after both sides are cooked
